Description of the method
„The purpose is not training uniformly perfect dogs, but consciously developing our relationship with our dog and understanding him, understanding the reasons of things.”

The human who lived in nature, the shepherd, the indian never had to bother about their dog. It is hard to imagine Winnetou chasing his own dog around the Mossy Rock, or old Uncle John yelling at his sheepdog, because he is not coming back. How is it possible and what has changed ever since...?

Mirror Method was born in Népsziget Dog School, as a result of several years of research. It is named after the mirror theory: our dog, like a mirror shows what kind of owners we are, and what we should develop. Only we have to be able to recognize these reflections.

The Mirror Method with its three sections makes an effort to make man and dog connection simple and understandable, step by step. It is divided into 3 main parts:

1.) Regulate relations with the help of instinct handling techniques – this subject matter is mostly based on our school's researches and we have already reached fantastic results in this matter. By handling instincts we can get to know the dog's individuality , instincts, we can understand the sources of problems of living together, and we can learn how to deal with them. Basically the owners learn how to develop their relationship with the dog, how to communicate in the dog's own language, how to make the dog understand and admit his place and how we can become the leader that the dog is looking for, to whom his predecessors handed over control.

2.) Training – a variety of methods exist, the most effective currently known of these is the clicker training, which our school mastered among the firsts in Hungary and had a great role in spreading it. The first clicker book in Hungarian language has been published under the school's editorship. Clicker training omits every kinds of violence and force while teaching and can be adjusted with instinct handling brilliantly. Therefore common work is a great adventure for both man and dog, develops the quality of man-dog relationship, communication between them, and the dog's learning ability grows to such an extent that we
can teach anything to him, with not more than the average energy input.

3.) Lifestyle – it is about how we can preserve our dog's health, how we can provide him a balanced, whole life. What belongs here: everything from feeding and walking habits to keeping our dog occupied. These days dogs swelling of energy get in the city and mostly they don't get useful tasks, because all we want from them is to be our companion, our friend. This way the hidden abilities, instincts, energies within them cannot be let out, and those flowing in them can make our dogs ill. That is the reason why our dogs catch illnesses much more easily than before. The solution is providing appropriate lifestyle for them, where we make our dog work phisically, mentally and instinctively, with a big walk in the woods, making them swim, dog sports or preparing them to an exam or a competition etc.

The good owner is conscious, consistent and cartoon porn keeps the principle of progressivity.
