The Mirror Group
The Mirror Group has been created soon after naming the method itself. Its primary role is processing incoming remarks, developing and testing the method, making conveyance of knowledge more effective. The Group has currently five active members, and three consultants help their work at certain fields.

Getting into the Group is possible only by invitation and every members' opinion is equally important. In active periods we meet once in a week and debate actual questions.

We find it important to represent our approach not only with dogs, but in every fields of life we are trying to live accordingly. Therefore, in case of any failure we don't blame anybody else, but searching for solutions in ourselves.
